The Home Turing Test

The Home Turing Test measures your ability to discern between human generated art and art generated by artificial intelligence.

10 questions will prompt you to respond 'True' or 'False' when asked whether the artwork is created by artificial intelligence.

And finally, we'll measure whether your perception toward artificial intelligence changes after taking the quiz.
